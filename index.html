"Adverse Events" in California Hospitals

An "adverse event" is when something goes wrong with a patient in a hospital. It can be as commonplace as bedsores, or as dangerous as "retention of a foreign object" after surgery.

Hospitals are required to report these events soon after they happen. Many states, like Washington or Minnesota, publish these numbers regularly. California puts these statistics in a single paper report once per year. The Investigative Unit had to file multiple Public Records Act Requests to find out which hospitals .
